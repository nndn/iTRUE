Dr. Milap Shah
MBBS, MS, MCh (Urology)
Resident: Urology, Kasturba Medical College, Manipal Manipal Academy of Higher Education, Manipal, India
Milap has presented various posters and papers at national and international conferences. He believes smart, affordable technology can revolutionize the health care sector. He has been part of the team developing health care applications to provide better patient care. He has keen interest in research and has authored several publications. His current research interests include outcomes research, systematic reviews in uro-oncology and artificial intelligence applications in Urology.
Outside of his studies, Milap loves football, cricket, movies and travelling.
